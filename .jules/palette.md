## 2024-05-23 - [Testing Radix UI Dropdown Interactions]\n**Learning:** Testing Radix UI primitives like `DropdownMenu` in JSDOM requires dispatching `pointerDown` before `click` on the trigger, and ensuring `ResizeObserver` and `DOMRect` are mocked globally.\n**Action:** When testing Radix components, always verify these mocks exist in `setup.ts` and use `fireEvent.pointerDown(trigger); fireEvent.click(trigger);` for interactions.
