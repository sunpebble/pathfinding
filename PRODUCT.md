# Product

## Register

product

## Users

People planning personal trips who want a calm place to collect itineraries, places, reminders, expenses, and AI-assisted drafts across web and iOS.

## Product Purpose

Sunpebble Trips is a focused itinerary tool under the Sunpebble product family. It should help users turn loose travel ideas into editable plans without depending on unclear third-party guide feeds.

## Brand Personality

Small, polished, calm. The interface should feel practical and careful, with Sunpebble ownership visible in naming, service URLs, and product copy.

## Anti-references

Do not present the product as a public guide aggregator, crawler console, or adventure-explorer portal. Avoid unclear guide-source surfaces unless the source can be shown completely and responsibly.

## Design Principles

- Keep the first screen task-oriented: continue planning, organize places, check service status.
- Treat AI as drafting support, not an authority.
- Remove or hide content whose source cannot be displayed clearly.
- Use Sunpebble service identity consistently across web, iOS, widgets, and sharing.
- Favor restrained product UI over decorative travel imagery.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast for user-facing surfaces, preserve keyboard focus states, and keep motion optional or state-driven.
